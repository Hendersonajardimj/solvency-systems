## Risk Mitigation

### If Upwork Isn't Working (Week 3)
**Pivot:**
- Try different service positioning
- Lower rates temporarily
- Focus on different categories
- Consider Toptal, Gun.io alternatives

### If SaaS Is Delayed (Week 6)
**Options:**
- Reduce scope, ship MVP
- Pivot to simpler product
- Double down on client work
- Launch info product instead

### If Content Isn't Growing (Week 4)
**Adjust:**
- Analyze what's working for others
- Change content mix
- Engage more with community
- Consider paid promotion

### If Burnout Risk (Ongoing)
**Prevention:**
- Strict time boundaries
- Say no to low-value work
- Automate more
- Take weekend off
